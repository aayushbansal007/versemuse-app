VerseMuse

Privacy Policy

1. Overview

VerseMuse ("we", "our", or "the App") is an AI-powered poetry assistant designed with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data, poems, or inputs with any external servers.

  • Local Processing: All poetry generation is performed locally on your device using an embedded AI model. Your creative work never leaves your phone.
  • Local Storage: Your saved poems are stored in a local database (SQLite/LocalStorage) on your device. We do not have access to this database.

3. Internet Access and Model Download

The App requires internet access only for the following specific purpose:

Initial AI Model Download

Upon first launch (or if the cache is cleared), the App connects to the internet to download the AI model weights (approximately 200MB) from Hugging Face/CDN. Once this download is complete, the App functions entirely offline.

4. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required solely to download the AI model files during the initial setup.

5. Children's Privacy

VerseMuse does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. Since the App operates offline and does not require account registration, no user data is transmitted to us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
